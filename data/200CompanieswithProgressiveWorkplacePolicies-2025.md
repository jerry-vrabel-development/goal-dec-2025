# 200 Companies with Progressive Workplace Policies - 2025

## Introduction

This list focuses on companies with progressive workplace policies including generous parental leave, comprehensive healthcare, environmental commitments, flexible work arrangements, and strong employee benefits that go beyond industry standards.

**Key Criteria:**
- Generous parental leave (12+ weeks paid)
- Comprehensive healthcare including mental health
- Strong environmental/sustainability commitments
- Flexible work policies
- LGBTQ+ inclusive benefits
- Caregiver and medical leave policies
- Employee wellness programs

## Key Progressive Policies to Look For

### Parental Leave
- **Excellent:** 16+ weeks paid for all parents
- **Good:** 12-16 weeks paid
- **Industry Standard:** 6-12 weeks
- **Look for:** Phased return, flexibility in timing, adoption/foster inclusion

### Healthcare
- **Comprehensive mental health** coverage
- **Gender affirmation** surgery and care
- **Fertility treatments** and family building
- **Abortion access** or travel coverage
- **Preventive care** with no copays
- **Dependent coverage** including domestic partners

### Flexibility
- **Remote or hybrid options**
- **Flexible hours**
- **Compressed workweeks**
- **Sabbatical programs**
- **Unlimited or generous PTO**

### Family Support
- **Backup childcare**
- **Elder care assistance**
- **Caregiver leave** beyond FMLA
- **Adoption assistance**
- **College tuition** for children

### Financial Wellness
- **Student loan repayment**
- **401(k) matching** 4%+
- **Employee stock programs**
- **Financial counseling**
- **Housing assistance**

### Professional Development
- **Tuition reimbursement**
- **Conference attendance**
- **Skill development budgets**
- **Internal mobility**
- **Mentorship programs**

## How to Research Benefits

1. **Company careers pages** - Benefits sections
2. **Glassdoor reviews** - "Benefits" tab and reviews
3. **Comparably** - Benefits ratings and comparisons
4. **Levels.fyi** - Compensation + benefits data
5. **LinkedIn** - Ask current employees
6. **During interviews** - Ask specific questions

## Questions to Ask

- "What does your parental leave policy include for all types of parents?"
- "How does the company support work-life balance in practice?"
- "What mental health resources are available?"
- "How are benefits communicated and accessed?"
- "What's the policy on remote/flexible work?"
- "Are there any benefits you're particularly proud of?"
